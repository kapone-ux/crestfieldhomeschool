FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many A Levels do students take?+

Students typically take 3-4 A Levels, depending on their university goals and academic capacity. Most UK universities require 3 A Levels with specific grade requirements.

Can students retake A Levels?+

Yes, we support students who wish to retake A Levels to improve their grades. Our flexible program allows for focused revision and exam preparation.

Do A Levels prepare for specific careers?+

Yes, A Level subject choices can prepare students for specific university courses and careers. We provide guidance on subject selection based on career aspirations.

How are A Levels assessed?+

A Levels are primarily assessed through final examinations at the end of the two-year program. Some subjects may include coursework components.
