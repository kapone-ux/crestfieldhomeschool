Homeschooling in Nairobi and Kenya

Structured support for families choosing personalized learning at home, online, or through guided tuition.

A growing choice for Kenyan families

Homeschooling is steadily gaining popularity in Nairobi and across Kenya as families seek more flexible and personalized education options. Unlike traditional schooling, homeschooling allows parents and educators to tailor learning experiences to suit each child's strengths, pace, and interests.

At Crestfield Tuition & Homeschool, learners can receive structured academic guidance, curriculum planning, subject tuition, exam preparation, and regular progress feedback while maintaining the flexibility that makes homeschooling valuable.

This approach works well for students who need a calmer learning environment, international curriculum support, catch-up lessons, advanced study, or a schedule that fits family and learner needs.

Personal paceLearning plans can slow down, accelerate, or deepen based on the student.
Parent visibilityFamilies stay close to progress, assignments, strengths, and next steps.
Online or in-personSupport can be arranged around location, timetable, and curriculum choice.
