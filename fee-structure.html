06 School Fee Policies
  1. Admission fee is inclusive of interview fee. A one-time Non-refundable admission fee is payable for the student enrolling for the first time.
  2. Payment of fee should be made before the commencement of classes.
  3. Soft copy books are compulsory for all fulltime online students
  4. We offer a 6 % one-time discount of tuition fees on siblings
  5. Failure to pay fees on time and students with fee balances will not be allowed to attend classes
  6. Students will not be allowed to roll over fee balances to the next term
  7. Any payments made will first be allocated to tuition and science practical fee where applicable
  8. Field trips and International Examination Fees which is done at Year 6, Year 9, Year 11-13 is invoiced and payable separately from the normal school fees
  9. If a student wishes to withdraw from the school, one term’s advance notice of withdrawal must be given in writing. School clearance will not be possible until any outstanding dues are settled in full
  10. The school reserves the right to review the school fees annually with regards to the effect of economy fluctuations on the cost of living
