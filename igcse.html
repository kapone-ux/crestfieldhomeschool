Program Overview

Crestfield's IGCSE program offers both Cambridge and Pearson Edexcel tuition pathways, giving students the flexibility to choose subjects that match their interests and future goals. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many subjects do students take?+

Students typically take 5-9 IGCSE subjects, including core subjects (Mathematics, English, Science) and optional subjects based on their interests and future plans.

What's the difference between Cambridge and Pearson Edexcel?+

Both are internationally recognized. Cambridge tends to be more traditional, while Pearson Edexcel may offer more coursework options. We help students choose the best fit.

Can students take exams in Kenya?+

Yes, we coordinate with approved exam centers in Kenya for IGCSE examinations. We provide full support with exam registration and preparation.

How long does the IGCSE program take?+

IGCSE is typically a two-year program (Years 10-11), but flexible pacing is available for students who need to accelerate or extend their studies.
