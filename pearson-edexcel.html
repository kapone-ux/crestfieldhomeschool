FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Cambridge and Pearson Edexcel?+

Both are internationally recognized. Pearson Edexcel often includes more coursework options, while Cambridge is more exam-focused. We help students choose based on their learning style.

Are Pearson Edexcel qualifications recognized?+

Yes, Pearson Edexcel qualifications are recognized by universities and employers in over 70 countries, including the UK, US, Canada, Australia, and Kenya.

Can students mix Cambridge and Edexcel subjects?+

Yes, many students take a combination of Cambridge and Pearson Edexcel qualifications. Both are equally recognized and valued by universities.

What resources are provided?+

Students receive access to Pearson's digital textbooks, online learning platforms, practice materials, and past papers, plus Crestfield's additional teaching resources.
