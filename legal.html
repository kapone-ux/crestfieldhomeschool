Privacy Policy

Crestfield International Academy respects the privacy of students, parents, teachers, applicants, and website visitors. We collect only the information needed to respond to enquiries, process applications, coordinate tuition or homeschool support, and improve communication.

  • Information may include names, contact details, student level, curriculum interests, subject needs, and submitted application details.
  • Information is used for admissions, academic support, consultation scheduling, communication, and service administration.
  • We do not sell personal information to third parties.
  • Submitted information should be accurate and shared by a parent, guardian, eligible student, or authorized applicant.

Data Protection Information

Crestfield International Academy aims to handle personal data responsibly, securely, and only for clear educational, administrative, or communication purposes.

  • Access to submitted information is limited to appropriate staff or authorized service support.
  • Data may be retained where needed for admissions, academic administration, records, compliance, or communication follow-up.
  • Parents, guardians, students, and applicants may request correction of inaccurate information through official contact channels.
  • Reasonable safeguards are used to protect submitted information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Website Usage Policies

Visitors must use this website respectfully, lawfully, and without attempting to interfere with its operation, forms, communication tools, or linked services.

  • Do not submit false, harmful, abusive, or unauthorized information through website forms.
  • Do not copy school branding, logos, images, or written content for misleading or unauthorized use.

For questions about privacy, data, or website policies, contact the school through the official phone, email, or WhatsApp details listed on the main website.
